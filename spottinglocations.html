East Midlands Airport Spotting Locations

TIP - The runway number corresponds to the runway heading. For example, runway 27 has a heading of 270 degrees and runway 09 has a heading of 090 degrees.

Airport Trail (Spot 1)

Where is it? This spot is located on the northern side of the airport roughly 0.6 miles or the equivalent of an 11 minute walk along the Airport Trail Path which follows the airfield boundary along the northern side of the airport.

How do I get there? Firstly, you need to reach East Midlands Gateway. To do this by car, exit the A453 at East Midlands Gateway and park along the inclined road leading up to the crash gate. By bus, take the Skylink Express, Skylink Nottingham or Skylink Leicester/Derby to East Midlands Gateway (4th stop from the terminal). Once you are there, follow the Airport Trail Path along the northern perimeter for roughly 11 minutes (0.6 miles). It is possible to walk to this spot from the terminal, however it would take upwards of an hour to reach and so is not recommended.

What will I see? At this spot you will see aircraft touching down on runway 27 as well as departures from runway 09. You will also have a view of the Eastern Apron as well as aircraft taxiing to/from the runway.

Additional Info: This spot is best for lighting on Summer evenings after 17:00 and summer mornings before 09:00.
A ladder is useful here since the fence is high, however the holes in the fence are large enough to take pictures through using most cameras.
A focal length of 70-300mm would best suit this spot.
The nearest restrooms are at the East Midlands Gateway bus stop and the nearest shops are at the main terminal itself.
